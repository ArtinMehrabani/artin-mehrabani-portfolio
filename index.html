Identifying Differentially Methylated Sites for Methylation-Sensitive qPCR-Based NIPT of Trisomies 13 and 18

Preview of published article (Image 1 & 2 from your upload)

Published IF: 1.0

Journal: Journal of Advanced Biomedical Research (2025)

Authors: Ali Najafi, Erfaneh Dastbaz, Artin Mehrabani, Morteza Rajabi, Ghazal Sedaghat Shayegan, Mohammad Mirvounesi, Mohammad Hossein Modarressi

My Contribution: Data Curation (equal), Investigation (supporting), Writing – Review & Editing (supporting)

Abstract: This study investigates methylation-sensitive quantitative PCR (MS-qPCR) as a cost-effective alternative to NGS-based NIPT for detecting trisomies 13 and 18. Twenty-two differentially methylated sites were successfully validated with high specificity and reproducibility.

Association Between First Trimester of Pregnancy Medication Use and Fetal DNA Fraction in Maternal Plasma

Manuscript preview (Image 3 & 4 from your upload)

Under Review IF: 6.3

Journal: Journal of Ultrasound in Obstetrics and Gynecology (Expected Jul 2025)

Authors: Artin Mehrabani, Ali Najafi, Sarang Younesi, Mehran Habibi-Rezaei, Mohammad Hossein Modarressi

My Contribution: Investigation (lead), Conceptualization (equal), Data Curation (equal), Writing – Original Draft (equal)

Abstract: Analyzed data from 43,722 pregnant women to determine the effect of first trimester medications on fetal fraction rates. Results show marked decreases in fetal fraction with drugs such as aspirin, enoxaparin, and levothyroxine, demonstrating strong associations between pharmacological exposure and fetal fractions.

Data Source: Genome-Nilou Laboratory, Tehran, Iran
